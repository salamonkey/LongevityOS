# Customer Input

Problem statement:
Care teams struggle to coordinate treatment plans and follow-ups across channels.

Intended outcome:
Provide a single workflow that captures patient plans, tasks, and status so teams can reduce delays.

Target users:
- Care coordinator
- Clinical operations lead

MVP scope:
- Create and update care plans
- Assign and track follow-up tasks
- Provide status visibility per patient and team

Constraints:
- Keep MVP web-first
- Preserve audit history
- Avoid external integrations in MVP
